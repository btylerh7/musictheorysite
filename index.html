What is Music Theory?

Music theory is not rocket science, by any means. However, it is difficult for people who are just starting out to master. Have no fear, though! I am here to fix that for you. In the following paragraphs, I will introduce you to music theory!

Think of music theory as the "language of music." Learning music theory is a lot like learning a language: you have to start with the basics and then expand. In lanugage, you have words. Words make sentences. In music, these words are called notes. Notes are essentially just sounds.
